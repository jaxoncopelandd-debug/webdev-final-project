My Golf Story

Golf has taught me patience, focus, and resilience. Every round is a chance to restart, learn from the last shot, and make better decisions on the next one.

Practice Plan (Nested Lists)

Weekly Practice Routine

  1. Warm-Up
    • 5 minutes of stretching
    • 10 short chip shots
    • 10 putts from 3–5 feet
  2. Full Swing
    • 15 irons focusing on tempo
    • 10 drives aiming for fairway
    • 10 approach shots to the green
  3. Short Game
    • Bunker practice from different lies
    • Pitching from 30–60 yards
    • Lag putting from 20–40 feet

Common Mistakes

  • Rushing the Swing
    1. Skipping pre-shot routine
    2. Taking a quick, jerky backswing
  • Poor Course Management
    1. Going for “hero shots” instead of smart layups
    2. Ignoring wind, hazards, and elevation changes
  • Mental Game Issues
    1. Getting stuck on past mistakes
    2. Playing scared instead of confident
